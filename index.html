Retired Clarkstown Resident Starts Salad Dressing Company

Pop Pop's Best is available so far in Congers, Nyack, Pearl River and New City stores. When he retired a bit less than two years ago, Lee Licht wasn’t sure if he wanted to kick back and relax. Instead, he started a business. “I was urged by my family to come out and market a salad dressing that I had been making for them and for my friends for years,” he said. “So, basically, that’s what I did.” Read more
