Avocado Egg Salad Recipe

Add a creamy twist to traditional egg salad with this versatile recipe. Ingredients (serves 4 people)

  1. 4 hard-boiled eggs, chopped
  2. 2 Roma tomatoes, seeded and chopped
  3. 1/4 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese
  4. 1/2 small onion, minced
  5. 1 small ripe avocado, quartered
  6. 2 tablespoons reduced-fat mayonnaise
  7. 1 tablespoon skim milk
  8. 2 teaspoons lime juice
  9. 1 dash taco sauce
  10. Salt and pepper, to taste
  11. Romaine lettuce (optional)
    12. Suggested toppings: additional cheese, chopped tomatoes, fresh cilantro
Directions Combine eggs, tomatoes, cheese and onion in a medium-sized bowl and mix well. Combine avocado, mayonnaise, milk, lime juice and taco sauce in a food processor or blender and process until smooth. Add avocado mixture to egg mixture in your bowl and stir to combine. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve over Romaine lettuce with additional toppings as desired inside a tortilla or flatbread. If you prefer, serve as a dip with pita chips.

Fresh Watermelon Salad Recipe

Mix up your summer salads with juicy watermelon, feta, pine nuts and balsamic dressing. Ingredients (serves 4 people)

  1. 4 cups seedless watermelon, diced in 1/2- to 3/4-inch pieces
  2. 1/2 cup (4 ounces) feta cheese, crumbled
  3. 1/4 cup pine nuts, toasted
  4. 2 tablespoons fresh mint, chopped
  5. 2 tablespoons white balsamic vinegar, or more to taste
  6. Freshly ground black pepper, to taste
Directions Combine watermelon, pine nuts and white balsamic in a large bowl and toss. Top with crumbled feta cheese, mint and ground pepper. Transfer to plates and serve immediately.
