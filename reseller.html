Fresh Watermelon Salad Recipe

Mix up your summer salads with juicy watermelon, feta, pine nuts and balsamic dressing. Ingredients (serves 4 people)

  1. 4 cups seedless watermelon, diced in 1/2- to 3/4-inch pieces
  2. 1/2 cup (4 ounces) feta cheese, crumbled
  3. 1/4 cup pine nuts, toasted
  4. 2 tablespoons fresh mint, chopped
  5. 2 tablespoons white balsamic vinegar, or more to taste
  6. Freshly ground black pepper, to taste
Directions Combine watermelon, pine nuts and white balsamic in a large bowl and toss. Top with crumbled feta cheese, mint and ground pepper. Transfer to plates and serve immediately.
